SDG Goals

Five key objectives that drive our mission forward

Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being

Through health insurance recommendation chatbot enables people to obtain cost-effective medical care with financial protection during a medical crisis.

Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

The project helps develop digital tools along with AI solutions that establish prospective markets for both innovation and job generation and technology-driven economic expansion.

Goal 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

InsureWise applies modern technologies (AI, NLP, and web development) to improve digital infrastructure and accessibility in the insurance sector.

Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities

The insurance access simplification provided by chatbot enables equitable insurance services independent of user literacy levels or technological knowledge.
